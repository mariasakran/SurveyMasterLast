About Our Project

Survey Master is a comprehensive digital survey system that empowers organizations and individuals to create professional surveys, collect responses, and gain valuable insights through powerful analytics.

Key features include:

  • Three-tier user system (Founder, Registered Users, Regular Users)
  • Survey creation with multiple voting options
  • Content categorization and personalized notifications
  • Anonymous response collection
  • Time-limited surveys with detailed analytics
  • Founder approval system for survey publication

Whether you're conducting academic research, market analysis, or customer feedback collection, Survey Master provides the tools you need to gather and interpret data effectively.

Survey Master is dedicated to providing the most efficient survey creation and analysis platform for professionals and organizations.
